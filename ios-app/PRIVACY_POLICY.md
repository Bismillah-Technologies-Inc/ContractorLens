# Privacy Policy for ContractorLens

**Last Updated:** December 14, 2025

## 1. Introduction
ContractorLens ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your data when you use our iOS application.

## 2. Data Collection and Usage

### 2.1 Camera and LiDAR Data
**Data Collected:** TrueDepth Camera data, LiDAR depth data, and camera images.
**Purpose:** To generate 3D room models and identify construction materials (e.g., "drywall", "hardwood").
**Storage:** 
- **On-Device:** All 3D models and raw scan data are processed and stored locally on your device.
- **Cloud Processing:** If you use the "AI Estimate" feature, specific image frames are temporarily sent to our secure servers for analysis by Google Gemini. These images are **not** stored permanently on our servers and are deleted immediately after analysis.

### 2.2 Location Data
**Data Collected:** Coarse location (City/Zip Code).
**Purpose:** To provide accurate, location-specific material and labor pricing (e.g., labor rates in New York vs. Texas).
**Storage:** Stored locally on your device.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Google Gemini (AI Provider):** For the sole purpose of analyzing construction materials in images you explicitly scan.
- **Firebase (Auth & Analytics):** To authenticate your account and monitor app stability (crashes).

## 4. Your Rights
You may delete your local data at any time by deleting the project within the app or uninstalling the application.
